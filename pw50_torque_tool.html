📏 General Torque Guidelines by Bolt Size (Yamaha Rule of Thumb)

Yamaha follows general torque guidelines based on bolt diameter and thread pitch, especially when a specific torque spec isn’t listed in the service manual.

Bolt Size Standard Torque (Nm) Notes
M42–3 NmVery small — used in electronics and covers
